About Kaizen Glass

Our story, our passion

Our Journey

Founded in 2005, Kaizen Glass began as a small workshop dedicated to preserving the ancient art of glassblowing while infusing it with contemporary design sensibilities.

The name "Kaizen" reflects our philosophy of continuous improvementâ€”each piece we create benefits from the knowledge and experience gained from all that came before it.

What started as a passion project by master glassblower Hiroshi Tanaka has grown into an internationally recognized studio of artisans dedicated to pushing the boundaries of glass art.
